The broader Universe
Despite centuries of scientific exploration and discovery, there are still unexplained phenomena in our universe that continue to puzzle and intrigue scientists. These include things like Black Holes, and things like the Bootes Void: an unexplainable absence of things. These phenomena challenge our understanding of the universe and push us to explore new frontiers of knowledge and discovery.
